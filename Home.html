Bio

I am a third-year university student from School of Interactive Arts and Technology, SFU. I had experience with Graphic design, UX design, and UI design. Following the design principle and care about user is my main goal as a designer. I always think about how the audience would see and interact to my design. I care about the details and consider the motion design of affordances to be crucial in creating effective.
